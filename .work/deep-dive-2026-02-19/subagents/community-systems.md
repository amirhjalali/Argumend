# Community Systems Output

## Governance Insight
Without transparent challenge and revision pathways, confidence scores risk being read as technocratic verdicts.

## Required System Additions
- Evidence challenge queue with public adjudication logs.
- Contributor reputation tied to calibration quality, not rhetorical popularity.
- Norm-violation and repair protocols for heated exchanges.
- Explicit handling for principled, persistent disagreement ("honorable dissensus" state).
