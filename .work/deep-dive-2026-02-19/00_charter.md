# Deep Dive Charter

## Objective
Develop a rigorous thesis and implementation plan for making Argumend the most compelling platform for structured disagreement, with the explicit mission of bridging understanding across polarized groups.

## Constraints
- Run 10 deliberate research/refinement loops.
- Produce at least 10 compelling, implementation-ready ideas.
- Ground recommendations in repository reality + external research.
- Maintain resumable state in files to avoid context-window loss.

## Non-goals
- Shipping code changes in this pass.
- Vague philosophy detached from product mechanics.

## Success Criteria
- Clear thesis tied to measurable product outcomes.
- Ranked ideas mapped to concrete architecture changes.
- Phased roadmap with risks, dependencies, and metrics.

## Method
- File-backed loop workflow with compressed handoffs.
- Role-based passes: repo-analyst, philosophy-analyst, product-strategist, community-systems.
