## Findings
- Communicative rationality is not “be nice”; it is a claim-testing regime requiring reciprocal justifiability.
- Every argument implicitly carries multiple validity claims: factual truth, normative rightness, and speaker sincerity.
- Product mistake today: collapsing all disagreement into one score obscures claim-type mismatch.
- "Ideal speech" can’t be fully achieved online, but approximations can be designed: turn symmetry, transparency, revisability, and coercion checks.
