### fluoride-water-supplies

The 2015 Cochrane review did find fluoridation reduces caries (~25%); its caveat was about old/low-quality evidence and inequality data, not ineffectiveness. The maxed weights risk reading as if Cochrane undermined efficacy. A 2024 Cochrane update (post the items here) found smaller modern benefit — NEEDS LIVE CHECK but worth noting the 2015 item is now somewhat dated as "the" Cochrane verdict.
- [MED] `federal-court-ruling-2024` (Food & Water Watch v. EPA, `directness: 9`): the ruling found fluoride poses an "unreasonable risk" under TSCA and ordered EPA to regulate, but the court explicitly did NOT find fluoride at 0.7 mg/L is proven harmful — it ruled the risk could not be excluded. Description is mostly accurate but `directness: 9` overstates how directly a procedural TSCA finding speaks to the health-effect question.
- [LOW] Otherwise the safety pillar is genuinely balanced (NTP, Bashash, court ruling vs. 70-year safety data); not false-balanced. Bashash item correctly notes higher-than-US exposure.
